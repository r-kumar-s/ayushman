Frequently Asked

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our Ayurvedic treatments and consultation process.

How long does it take to see results from Ayurvedic treatment?
Results vary by condition and individual constitution. Typically, patients notice significant improvements within 4–8 weeks of consistent treatment. Chronic conditions like thyroid disorders often stabilize within 3–4 months, while acute symptoms like stress or pain relief can be experienced within the first few sessions.
Are there any side effects from Ayurvedic medicines?
When prescribed by a qualified Ayurvedic physician and sourced from pure, quality-tested herbs, Ayurvedic medicines have minimal to no side effects. All our formulations are prepared in-house using wild-harvested, tested herbs — absolutely no synthetic chemicals or fillers.
Can I continue my allopathic medications alongside Ayurvedic treatment?
Yes, in most cases patients can continue their existing medications during Ayurvedic treatment. Dr. Sushma Tiwary carefully reviews all current medications during consultation. Many patients are gradually able to reduce and eventually eliminate allopathic medications as their condition improves — but never abruptly and always under medical guidance.
Do I need to follow any dietary restrictions during treatment?
Dietary guidance is an integral part of Ayurvedic healing. Dr. Sushma will prescribe a personalized Pathya (compatible diet) based on your Prakriti (body type) and condition. The guidelines are practical and easy to follow — not extreme or restrictive.
Is Panchakarma treatment safe? Who should avoid it?
Panchakarma is safe when performed under the supervision of a trained Ayurvedic physician. Certain conditions such as pregnancy, severe anemia, or acute infections may require modifications or avoidance of specific procedures. Dr. Sushma conducts a thorough pre-assessment to determine the safest and most effective protocols for each individual.
Do you offer online consultations?
Yes, we offer online video consultations for patients who cannot visit the clinic in person. However, for Panchakarma, Shirodhara, and other hands-on therapies, you will need to visit our clinic in Satellite, Ahmedabad. Contact us to schedule an online consultation.
